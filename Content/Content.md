# Source Materials & References

## 1. Document Inventory
This directory contains the manifest of source documents used to validate the RAG pipeline. The file `Source.txt` lists the direct URLs to the University Prospectuses processed by this system.

### Institutions Referenced:
*   **BITS Pilani** (BITSAT 2025 Brochure)
*   **SRM Institute of Science and Technology** (2025-26 Prospectus)
*   **Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE)** (2024 Prospectus)
*   **Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT)** (VITEEE Prospectus)

## 2. Usage & Compliance
These documents were retrieved directly from the respective official university websites.
*   **Purpose**: These materials are utilized strictly for **educational research** and **demonstration** of the PDF extraction pipeline.
*   **Scope**: This project is non-commercial. The documents serve as high-complexity test cases (rich in diagrams, tables, and variety of layouts) to benchmark the extraction algorithms.

## 3. Acknowledgment
We acknowledge the availability of these public documents from MAHE, BITS, VIT, and SRM, which facilitated the testing and calibration of this multimodal RAG framework.
